## Data Accuracy Limitations

### Estimated Nutrient Values

**All nutrient data provided by this application is estimated and approximate.**

Actual nutrient content in foods varies based on:
- Growing conditions, soil quality, and farming methods
- Food variety and breed
- Storage conditions and freshness
- Cooking methods and preparation
- Brand and manufacturer differences
- Organic vs. conventional farming

**The App cannot account for these variations.** Nutrient values are derived from:
- USDA FoodData Central (public database)
- Manufacturer-provided information
- User-submitted data (where applicable)
- Scientific literature and reference sources

### User Input Accuracy

Nutrient analyses are only as accurate as the information you provide. The App relies on:
- Your honest and accurate logging of foods consumed
- Your estimation of portion sizes and serving amounts
- The quality of food database matches

**Inaccurate logging will result in inaccurate analyses.**
